Philly Cheesesteak


4 servings



Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 lb beef sirloin, sliced
  • 3 taplespoons of olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 3 ounces of swiss cheese, thinly sliced
  • 4 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Directions

  1. Mix seasonings in a small bowl
  2. Slice onion and greem peppers
  3. Place steak in a large bowl, and mix the seasonings and olive oil with the steak
  4. Heat skillet to over medium high heat
  5. Add steak, cook & stir to liking
  6. Move aside steak to a seperate bowl when cooked
  7. Heat the remaining oil in the skillet, add onion & green pepper
  8. Cook & stir until tender and carmelized
  9. Preheat oven to the broiler setting. Divide the cooked beef between 4 hoagie rolls
  10. Layer it with onion & green pepper
  11. Top it with sliced cheese, and place it in a cooking sheet
  12. Broil until cheese is melted
  13. Serve & Enjoy!
