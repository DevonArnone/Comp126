Steak and Hasselback Potatos


1 serving


__________________________________________________

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Steak
  • Black-pepper
  • Montreal-steak seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Wash and dry your potato
  3. Put your potato on a cutting board and place two chopsticks, skewers, or butter knifes on each long side of the potato
  4. Cut the potato crosswise into slice with your desired size (I suggest 1/8-1/16 of an ich slices)
  5. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter in the microwave until the butter is melted
  6. Add olive oil and salt into the butter bowl
  7. Brush this mixture on your sliced potato, focus on getting this mixture inbetween every slice
  8. Put the potato on a baking sheet and put it in the oven
  9. Cook the potato for 1 hour and 20 minutes, every 20 minutes reapplying this butter mixture on the potato
  10. In the last 20 minutes of the potato cooking, put a pan on the stove top and turn on the burner to medium high heat
  11. Put your steak on a cutting board and apply salt, black-pepper, and montreal-steak seasoning on both sides
  12. When the pan is hot, place the steak on the pan for whatever time is needed to cook your steak to the desired wellness
  13. Once steak is done, take it off the pan and put it back on the cutting board to cool for 5 minutes
  14. Serve & Enjoy!
