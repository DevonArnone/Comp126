American Cheeseburger


4 servings


__________________________________________________

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds 80/20 ground beef
  • Salt and pepper or burger seasoning blend
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion (optional toppings)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, etc.)

Directions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high and clean the grates if needed.
  2. Divide 2 pounds of 80/20 ground beef into four equal portions.
  3. Shape patties about ½ to ¾ inch thick and make a slight indentation in the center.
  4. Season each side with burger seasoning blend or salt and pepper.
  5. Grill patties for 3-5 minutes per side, depending on desired doneness.
  6. Add cheese slices during the last minute of cooking, if desired.
  7. Toast buns on the grill, if preferred.
  8. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, condiments, and the cooked patty.
  9. Serve and enjoy with your favorite sides!
